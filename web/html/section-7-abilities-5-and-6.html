Abilities — Ability 5 & 6

Extending our 'Ability row' to the right is not ideal.
While T is good key, Y is not.

But not every hero has Ability 5 or/and Ability 6.
And if they do, some abilities are very important and some are rarely used.
Some abilities are passives.

I believe I found a solution.

I'm going to start with a simple example:

Here we moved Fan of Knives from T to R, because Coup De Grace is a passive ability.

We just made it easier for outselves to use it.

Another example: Ogre Magi.

Multicast is a passive.
It can't be activated.

Here we moved Fire Shield to R.

This is reasonable, because both of our 'buff abilities' Bloodlust and Fire Shield are next to each other and use great keys.

> > Important! < <

You still need a hotkey for your passive abilities!

If you don't have a hotkey, you can't use CTRL + Ability Key to LEARN the ability.

You don't need a great key, but you do need a key.

Here I want to highlight something...

It is possible to bind abilities to control group keys!

Despite the fact that we have Control Group 4 bound to 4, we can use 4 as an ability key!

That's fantastic news, because most of the time we have no use for this many control groups, so keys like 3, 4 and 5 are essentially free real estate.

This means, Hoodwink — and many other heroes — can get away with 0 control groups.
