Preface

Now, don't worry — there'll be a plenty of images, but let me say something before we begin:

Your hotkeys should make sense.
They should be efforless.
Easy to remember.
Simple to use.

The more intuitive they are, the fewer mistakes you’ll make, and the faster you’ll be able to execute your decisions.

Even if your decisions aren’t great, at least your execution won’t be the limiting factor.

Think about it: your hotkey layout is the only way you interact with the game.

It's a direct bridge between your brain and your game.

But when you plug a device into a computer, you never want it to run slower, do you?

On this page, you’ll find a VERY detailed explanation of what I consider the best Dota 2 hotkeys, settings and options possible.

I’ll explain the logic behind each decision for every hotkey and every in-game setting.
