Shop & Courier

There are many different configurations for all-things-shop.
Here's the simplest way to go about it.

  • Select Courier: F1
  • Open Shop: Alt+1
  • Quickbuy: Alt+2
  • Courier Deliver: Alt+3
  • Courier Burst: Alt+4
  • Courier Shield: Alt+5
  • Buy Sticky: Y

In any case, you want the following pattern to be easy:
QuickBuy → Courier Deliver → Courier Boost

F1Select Courier is right next to our control groups.
This makes sense, because it's "Select Something" key.

YBuy Sticky is not used very often, but I prefer having hotkeys for everything.
This action used to be much more useful, before we weren't getting free Teleport Scrolls on deaths.

Uncheck these options:
Shop Always Uses Hotkeys
Shop Search Gets Focus On Open
Enable Simple Shop

A lot of keys don't work with Shop Search Gets Focus On Open enabled.
That makes sense, because the keys go straight into the search bar.
The worst part: it blocks the keys that can't even be typed, like F1 or Alt + F1.

I prefer my hotkeys working all the time, thank you.

Fun fact!

- "&" filters out Sange and Yasha, Kaya and Sange, Yasha and Kaya
- "1" filters out Diffusal Blade, Dagon 1 and Disperser
- "2" filters out Boots of Travel 2, Dagon 2
- "/" filters out Hurricane Pike, Dragon Lance, Kaya and Sange
- "(", ")" filters out Phase Boots, Power Threads
- "7", "#", "=", "<", ">" filters out Hurricane Pike and Dragon Lance
