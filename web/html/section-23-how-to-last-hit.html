How to Last-Hit

Step 1a
Enable Auto-repeat Right Mouse.

Step 1b
Enable Quick Attack.

Step 2
Hover you mouse cursor over the target you want to last-hit.

Step 3
Click and hold Right-Click on the target.
Attack won't work for this!
You have to use Right-Click!

Step 4
Spam-tap the Stop command to cancel the attack animation.

This way you can reliably last-hit most creeps.

Choose Right-Click Allies: To Attack for better denying early on.
Choose Right-Click Allies: To Deny for better gameplay. Right-Click Allies: To Attack attempts to deny the creep even if it's above 50% health.
The attack animation is repeatedly cancelled until the unit falls below 50% health.

This effectively 'locks' your hero on target at your attack range.

There are disadvantages:

1) You lose the "click-to-follow".
2) You have to use the Move button, which is less convenient.
3) Imagine a big fight and you need to run away. If you accidentally click on anything allied instead of ground - your hero will stop moving and turn in attempt to attack it.

This will surely get you killed, unless you click all things perfectly all the time.
That's why I do not recommend this.

If you choose Right-Click Allies: To Deny and the target can't be denied - your hero will walk toward it and follow it.

This changes how you "think" about denies, because now your hero isn't 'locked'.

And you can't 'pre-attack' to deny, which is a minor disadvantage.

However, there are major advantages:

1) You do not lose the "click-to-follow" ability.
2) You don't need the Move button.
3) If you accidentally click on an ally instead of ground - your hero will still move in the general direction you wanted.

Choose Right-Click Allies: To Attack for better denying early on.
Choose Right-Click Allies: To Deny for better gameplay.
