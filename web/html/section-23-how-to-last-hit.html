How to Last-Hit

Step 1a. Enable Auto-repeat Right Mouse.

Step 1b. Enable Quick Attack.

Step 1c. Check Right-Click Allies: To Attack (for denies).

Step 2. Hover you mouse cursor over the target you want to last-hit.

Step 3. Click and hold Right-Click.
Attack won't work for this! Use Right-Click!

Step 4. Spam-tap the Stop command to cancel the attack animation.

This way you can reliably last-hit most creeps.

The reason to choose Right-Click Allies: To Attack is for denying.

If you choose Right-Click Allies: To Deny your hero will walk toward the creep if it's above 50% or can't be denied.
If you choose Right-Click Allies: To Attack your hero will not walk toward the creep if it's above 50% or can't be denied.

This is a better option.
