Directional Move

Sometimes you want to turn your her towards a direction exactly.

That's why Directional Move is used all the time on heroes like Shadow Fiend, Slark, Ursa, Earth Spirit — it makes it easier to line up things.

There are 2 ways to bind it.

CapsLock is a great key for Directional Move, because it's pressed with the pinky finger, which allows you to press all the abilities and items as usual.

  • Directional Move: CapsLock

Not available through in-game settings, but
You can bind your Alt to your Right-Click in autoexec.cfg

  • bind "ALT" "+dota_directional_move"
  • cl_dota_alt_unit_movetodirection true
