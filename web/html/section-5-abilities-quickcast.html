Abilities — Quick Cast

Abilities that use Quick Casts fire as soon as you press the key.
Quick Casts skip the "Select Target" part of the casting.
That's why it's quicker.

As a rule of thumb, use Quick Casts for everything except Point Target Area of Effect spells like these: Black Hole, Nether Blast, Chronosphere, etc.

We need to carefully aim these abilities, so it's better to keep them on Normal Cast.

Don't forget to enable Quick Casts for Neutrals.
Most neutrals have an ability to cast.
