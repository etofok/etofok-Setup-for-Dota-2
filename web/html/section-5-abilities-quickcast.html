Abilities — Quick Cast

Abilities on Quick Casts fire as soon as you press the key.
You don't need to "Select Target".

As a rule of thumb, use Quick Casts for everything except Point Target Area of Effect spells like these: Black Hole, Nether Blast, Chronosphere, etc.

We need to aim these very carefully, so it's better to keep them on Normal Cast.

Don't forget to set Quick Casts for Neutrals.
Most neutrals have an ability to cast.
