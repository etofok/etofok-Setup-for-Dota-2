Abilities - No Target Abilities

IMPORTANT

How to bind No Target abilities like these:

Mirror Image, Rip Tide, Song of the Siren

or

Quas, Wex, Exort, Invoke

(and many other abilities)

Bind No Target abilities to both Normal Cast and Quick Cast.

If a No Target ability is bound to Q, Alt + Q will not fire it.

Spend a few minutes and bind No Target abilities like this:
Normal Cast — Q
Quick Cast — Alt + Q

This will come into play in a hectic fight.
